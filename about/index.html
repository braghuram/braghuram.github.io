Violin Maestro B Raghuram was born to Vidwan Harmonium S. Bheema Rao and Smt Rajamma in 1960, Bangalore. Harmonium and violin maestro 'Shruthi-Laya Visharada' S. Bheema Rao is a disciple of Mysore Palace Asthana Vidwan Bidaram Krishnappa. He accompanied on violin and harmonium, the great stalwarts of those days like Bidaram Krishnappa, H Muthaiah Bhagavatar etc. He has few great compositions to his name which are being rendered regularly even today. Sri B Raghuram started violin at the age of 5 under the guidance of his father. He was very quick in grasping the intricacies of the music and mastering the techniques of playing on violin and harmonium.

He gave his first concert at the age of 10.

Today, Sri B Raghuram regularly accompanies top artistes both on violin and Harmonium. It is not uncommon for him sometimes to carry both violin and harmonium to a concert and play what ever the main artiste wants him to accompany with. His depth of knowledge and the ability to bring out the essence of a raga is greatly appreciated by scholars and listeners alike. The level of command he has on the laya aspect of music is very rare to find. No wonder he is a very sought out artiste.

He is highly knowledgeable yet very humble person. Students/learners just flock over to him. He is so flexible in accommodating students from all age groups (he has students with ages between 5 to 70yrs) irrespective of their financial/social position. All that takes one to learn music from him is the interest and dedication towards music. He has several of his students playing violin professionally today. B Raghuram is an accomplished composer as well. His style resembles that of an authentic Mysore-Baani. Many recognitions and laurels came his way. To name a few:

1) Won First-prize in All India Radio (AIR) Music Competition in 1983
2) Awarded as 'Best Musician' by Bangalore Gayana Samaja in 1984
3) Accomplished A-Grade artiste in All India Radio (AIR) and Doordarshan
4) He was conferred many titles like

a) Nadajyothi Puraskar
b) Guru Kalabhushana
c) Nadashri
d) Nadakala Praveena

and many more.

Sri B Raghuram founded a Music Trust by name 'Vidwan Harmonium S. Bheemarao Trust' and thorough this he promotes music awareness among the people. This trust regularly conducts concerts. It encourages upcoming artistes by providing them an opportunity to display their musical skills.

His daughters Kum. Ramya and Kum. Divya themselves are child prodigies in the classical music field. Both of them received CCRT scholarship. They are getting advanced training in violin from their father.
